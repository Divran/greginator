Step 1

This setup allows you to automatically change which circuit config, lens, extruder shape, or whatever, that your machines can see - in a compact, and hopefully performant, way.

Credits to Phineasor on GTNH Discord for the original idea

  1. Build an ME chest and put an item storage cell in it.
  2. Place a storage bus both on the front face (the one with the light) and on any other face.
  3. Place a level emitter on the cable which is attached to the front face pointing at an inverted toggle bus (see image below).
  4. Configure the level emitter according to the image below. Set the item to an empty storage cell matching the one you placed in the ME chest, the mode to "above or equal", and the amount to 1.

Step 2

  1. Build a second ME chest according to the image below. Connect the level emitters via a one-way connection using a storage bus and interface.
  2. Configure the bottom level emitter similarly to the first in step 1, except increment the amount to 2.
  3. Configure the top level emitter inversely, setting its mode to "below" and its amount to 1.

Step 3

  1. Extend this as far as you want, incrementing the amount in the level emitters by 1 each time you do.
    The amounts are shown in the image. The mode of each level emitter is described in step 2.
  2. Add chests with any lenses, circuits, extruder shapes, etc that your machines need.

Step 4

You are now done.

Explanation:

  1. The 1st level emitter you placed will simply turn on the toggle bus when it detects any items in its adjacent inventory, due to the fact that once the storage cell is no longer empty, the level emitter sees that you have zero empty storage cells
  2. The 2nd and 3rd level emitters you placed work together to tell the next toggle bus in the chain to only activate if all of the ME chests to the left are empty and this specific ME chest isn't empty.
  3. This setup is then repeated as far as you want, and the result is that only a single ME chest's toggle bus can be active at any given time, with priority going to the leftmost ME chest.

Items placed in 2nd and 3rd chests, only 2nd chest is active.

Items removed from 2nd chest, 3rd chest activates instead.
